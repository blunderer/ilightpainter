iLightPainter is a simple Application for those who like to make photography.

It allow you to choose between four different modes:

- Strobe: Will strobe using <color> at <frequency>

- Text: Will flash letters from <text> for a total time of <period> sec

- Rainbow: Will display a color gradient going from <color> to <color> in <period> sec

- Color: Will illuminate screen with <color>

When settings are ok, just press bottom buttons for choosing mode.
Then you will just have to touch screen to begin painter.
Touching screen again will go back to main menu.

In order to have the best results, you should increase brigthness in your setting panel.
Be carefull, when application is in painter mode, no sleep mode will occur.

Enjoy!
